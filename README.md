#Sales Performance Dashboard – Tableau

Developed an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard in Tableau to analyze and monitor key business metrics, including sales revenue, shipment trends, product performance, salesperson efficiency, and country-wise sales distribution. Cleaned and transformed sales data to create dynamic visualizations, KPI indicators, and interactive reports that provide a comprehensive view of business performance. Conducted in-depth analysis to identify top-selling products, high-performing sales representatives, shipment patterns, and regional sales trends. Leveraged filters, drill-down capabilities, and dashboard interactivity to enable data-driven decision-making and uncover actionable insights for improving sales strategies and operational efficiency.

Key Skills: Tableau, Data Visualization, Dashboard Development, Data Analysis, KPI Reporting, Business Intelligence, Sales Analytics, Interactive Dashboards, Data Storytelling.
